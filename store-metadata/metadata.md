## App Description
**You're the cleaner. No questions asked.**

When the job is done, someone has to make it disappear. That someone is you. Armed with mops, brushes, trash bags, and more — race against the clock to scrub away every trace of evidence before time runs out.

**FEATURES:**
- 25 thrilling levels across 3 environments: Apartments, Warehouses & Offices
- 5 specialized cleaning tools — each designed for specific evidence types
- Upgrade your tools to clean faster, reach further, and work harder
- Combo system — chain clean-ups for massive score multipliers
- Progressive difficulty with darkness, time rushes, and regrowing stains
- Stunning neon-noir cartoon visuals with immersive sound effects

**GAMEPLAY:**
Swipe and drag your tools across crime scenes to eliminate blood stains, broken glass, trash, evidence, and damaged furniture. Choose the right tool for each job — spray first, then scrub! Build combos to earn bonus points and unlock stars.

**UPGRADE & PROGRESS:**
Earn coins from completed missions to upgrade your cleaning arsenal. Boost tool power, reach, and speed to tackle the toughest scenes. Track your progress across all 25 cases.

Clean it. Hide it. Done.
